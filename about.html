kaetlyn mccafferty

I'm a bird-loving, garden-growing, self-taught Irish speaker, fiddle-player, and creative maniac who is driven by an insatiable curiosity and desire to learn.

Creativity has been one of the most important aspects of my life; creating work based on the world around me has been my means of understanding and processing it since childhood. In 2012, I received a BFA from the Cleveland Institute of Art with a major in drawing and a minor in photography. Following graduation, I began working in construction while doing freelance creative work and exploring new avenues. It's during that time that I came across web development, and there's been no looking back; the mixture of language, architecture, problem-solving, and aesthetics immediately clicked with me. In my spare time, I spent hours self-learning code with the help of sites like TeamTreehouse and Codecademy.

I'm currently studying Web Development Technologies at Lorain County Community College.

This website is currently a project of mine that will be frequently updated as I continue to learn new things. In the meantime, below you'll find some links to relevant profiles, my email, and you can click here to see my up-to-date CV. Let's get in touch!
