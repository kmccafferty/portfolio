kaetlyn mccafferty

My name's Kaetlyn. I'm a bird-and-horse-loving, Irish-speaking, garden-growing, must-make-everything creative maniac. I recieved a BFA in Drawing from the Cleveland Institute of Art in 2012. My strongest suit is working with wet media (particularly watercolor and gouache), but I know my way around an array of cameras and films as well. In 2014 I began learning web development and there's been no looking back; the intersection between problem-solving, architecture, language, and aesthetic was something that clicked straightaway for me.
